Our Services

We provide all general services related to two-wheelers. Our mehtods incorporate technology with smart choice of brands for products that deliver premium experience to our customers at highly affordable costs!

Bike Servicing

Includes filter cleaning, changing engine oil (recommended but optional) and brake oil, cleaning spark plug and every component of the bike, greasing and oiling all components of bike

Bike Wash

Bike cleaning using the eco-wash technique which involves very less or no water though with better results and cleansing muddy parts using water-saving pressure gun

Part Replacement

Replacement of the parts which are worn out or will need replacement soon like a spark plug, filter, brake wires, head and rear lamps, indicators, punctured/worn-out tube and tyres etc

Customization

We provide basic-utility bike customizations/modifications like adding fog LED lights, number plates, reflectors, wing mirrors, foot guard etc which are legit according to terms of RTO

Mishap service

Got troubled due to a minor accident or bike puncture? Have no worries contact us through our app or call, our mechanic will be there to your rescue at the spot of mishap

24/7 Support

We provide 24 by 7 roadside and highways assistance for any problems you may have on your journey from dusk to dawn we are always beside you as your GURU kam DOST
